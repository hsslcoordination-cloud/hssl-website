About

I am a newly graduated architect from Chalmers University of Technology, with a strong interest in thoughtful, context-driven design. My education provided a solid foundation in architectural theory, design development, and technical execution, with an international outlook at its core.

I have two years of professional experience working at Liljewall in Gothenburg and at Shigeru Ban Architects in Tokyo. During this time, I contributed to projects located in South Africa, Italy, Brazil, and Japan, gaining valuable experience in diverse cultural, climatic, and architectural contexts.
